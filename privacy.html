Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

1. Introduction

NEZO X ID ("we", "us", "our") operates a digital marketplace for the sale of Free Fire gaming accounts. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you interact with our website or contact us through WhatsApp, Instagram or our contact form, how we use that information, and the choices you have regarding it. By using our website or messaging our team, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you provide directly: When you contact us via WhatsApp, Instagram, or the contact form on this website, we may receive your name, phone number, social media handle, and any details you share about the account you're interested in or purchasing.
  • Transaction details: When you complete a purchase, we record the account ID, price, payment method, and date of the transaction for our internal records and for warranty purposes.
  • Automatically collected information: Like most websites, our site may collect basic technical information such as browser type, device type, and pages visited, primarily for performance and security purposes.
  • Theme and display preferences: If you toggle between dark and light mode, this preference is stored locally in your browser using localStorage and is not transmitted to our servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfil account purchases, including sending login credentials and security guidance after a sale.
  • Respond to inquiries, provide customer support, and handle warranty or refund requests.
  • Maintain records of transactions for accounting, dispute resolution, and fraud prevention.
  • Improve our website, listings, and overall service based on aggregated, non-identifying usage patterns.
  • Communicate updates about new listings, only if you have opted in via Instagram or WhatsApp broadcast lists.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. Limited information may be shared only in the following circumstances:

  • With payment processors or banking partners solely to verify and complete a transaction.
  • When required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of NEZO X ID, our customers, or others, including investigating fraud or security issues.

5. Data Retention

We retain transaction records for as long as necessary to fulfil warranty obligations, resolve disputes, and comply with applicable accounting or legal requirements. Casual inquiries that do not result in a transaction are retained only as long as needed to respond to your question, after which they may be deleted from our active records.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information shared with us, including limiting access to transaction records to authorized team members only. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission — including messaging apps such as WhatsApp — is completely secure. We encourage buyers to change all account credentials (email, password, and linked phone number) immediately after delivery to secure their new account.

7. Third-Party Platforms

Our marketplace relies on third-party platforms such as WhatsApp and Instagram for communication. Your use of these platforms is also subject to their respective privacy policies and terms of service, which we encourage you to review separately.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

9. Your Choices

You may choose not to provide certain information, though this may limit our ability to process your purchase or respond to your inquiry. You can also request that we delete personal information associated with a closed inquiry by contacting us, subject to our legal recordkeeping obligations for completed transactions.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when this policy was last revised. Continued use of our website or services after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
